What is MealSloth?

An app which allows you to buy and sell homemade food

MealSloth connects foodies and cooking enthusiasts to promote superior quality home cooked meals to everyone. With MealSloth, anyone can be a chef, and everyone has access to fresh homemade food!

Mission

Save Healthy Connect
Money is an important concern for people who enjoy eating out. We are here to help you save and even earn some extra cash Commercially made meals are mostly unhealthy. We want to make sure you get to enjoy homemade dishes. There is no open platform for people to buy and sell homemade dishes. We are here to connect you all.
