MealSloth

In the modern world, we all crave delicious meals and at MealSloth quality meals are our top priority. We have engineered a platform for connecting the common home chef with people who crave delicious home cooked meals.

We realize that no matter where we are, hearty home cooking is always the best, so we want to make it available to everyone. As a group of driven university students, the MealSloth team emphasizes not only the food itself, but also ensures everything from the production to the consumption of the food is taken care of.

We can provide a high level of safety, quality, and affordability by keeping directly in touch with the provider and consumers. At MealSloth, our customers are the most important part of the company, and we hope that you will be able to see that by your experience with us. We hope you can join us and find some of the best home cooked meals in your area! We believe sharing is caring, and will go to any extent to provide the sharing opportunity for you all.
