Frequently Asked Questions

What is MealSloth?

We are a mobile application platform (IOS, Android) for you to buy and sell homecooked meals and share your passion on food.

Is MealSloth free?

Yes! Our goal is to connect you and cooking enthusiast around your neighbou hood. You will only pay for the food price that's listed above by the cookers. We will not ask for any money from you. Start exploring now!

How safe is the food at MealSloth?

Indeed! We work closely to every home chef to ensure their kitchen sanitary and quality of the food. All of our chef will need to pass throuigh a list of screening process before becoming a MealSloth chef. We take quality and safety of our consumers very serious. Just like other sharing economy platform (Uber, Airbnb, etc). We go even further because we know how important it's to keep everyone safe and happy.

Can I sell on MealSloth even if I am not a professional chef?

Yes, we want you! We believe in passion and we know everyone can be a chef. No matter you are a student, part-time mother, retired folks or even just a working millennials; if you love cooking and sharing your home made foods with other, we are here to connect you to hundred of consumers around you. Just submit your interested form Here and we will get back to you very soon!

When will I be able to download the MealSloth app?

We have been working on this project for the past 5 months and we are committed to provide you the best food experience that you can get. Yes, we will launch our first beta this year. Connect with us now and sign up for our email newsletter for our latest update!
