Frequently Asked Questions

What is MealSloth?

We are a mobile application platform for iOS and Android where users can buy and sell homecooked meals in their local area and share their passion for food.

Is MealSloth free?

Yes! Our goal is to connect foodies and cooking enthusiasts around your neighborhood. You will only pay for the food price that's listed above your chosen meal. We will not ask for any additional money. Start exploring now!

How safe is the food at MealSloth?

We work closely with every home chef to ensure that each kitchen is sanitary and uses high quality food. All of our chefs participate in a screening process before becoming a MealSloth chef which performs checks on criteria equivalent to those in commercial restaurants. We take the safety of our consumers very seriously and provide as much screening as possible to ensure that our chefs take it as seriously as we do. Just like with other popular sharing economy platforms, our customers expect safety and we provide it.

Can I sell on MealSloth even if I am not a professional chef?

Yes, we want you! We believe that dedication and passion can enable anyone to be a chef. No matter if you are a student, part-time working mother, retired, or even a busy working millennial; if you love cooking and sharing your homemade food with others, we are here to connect you to hundreds of consumers around you. Just let us know your interest here and we will get more information for you very soon!

When will I be able to download the MealSloth app?

MealSloth has been working day and night for months, and we are committed to providing you the best food experience possible. We are excited to launch our first public beta this Spring! Connect with us now and sign up for our email newsletter to receive updates about our progress!
